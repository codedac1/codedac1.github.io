FloatTimer+

An interval, Pomodoro, and multi-timer, counter, and stopwatch that floats on top of every app.

View on Google Play → Contact us

Overview

FloatTimer+ is a floating overlay app that keeps an interval timer, multi-timer, counter, and stopwatch on top of any app you use. Your timer stays visible while you multitask, and you can drag it anywhere on screen while customizing its size, opacity, and background color. It runs in the background, so it keeps going even when you switch apps or lock the screen. Set it once and keep cooking, exercising, studying, or gaming without ever losing sight of your timer.

Key Features

  • Floating overlay — timers, counters, and a stopwatch float on any app, with per-item size, opacity, and background color
  • Multi-timer — run several custom hours/minutes/seconds countdowns at once and attach a memo to each
  • Interval timer — build Work/Rest phases with rounds for HIIT and Tabata, plus one-tap presets (Pomodoro, HIIT, Tabata, breaks)
  • Tally counter — tap-to-count floating counter with a custom step to add or subtract any amount (+1, +2, +5, +10…)
  • Floating stopwatch — start, pause, and reset without ever leaving your current app
  • Statistics, streaks, and backup — daily usage history and streaks, timer/counter file backup and restore, CSV export (Pro)

FAQ

Does the timer stay visible while I use other apps?

Yes. The timer, counter, and stopwatch float on top of any app and run in the background, so they keep going even when you switch apps or lock the screen.

Does it support interval timers and Pomodoro?

Yes. You can build custom Work/Rest phases with multiple rounds for HIIT and Tabata, and there are one-tap presets for Pomodoro, HIIT, Tabata, short break, and long break.

Can I run multiple timers or counters at once?

Running several timers and counters at the same time is a Pro feature. The free version still lets you use a single timer, counter, and stopwatch.

Clipboard+ for Windows

A Windows tray clipboard manager that syncs with your Android phone through your own Google Drive.

View in Microsoft Store → Contact us

Overview

Clipboard+ for Windows is a tray-resident clipboard manager that automatically records the text and images you copy. It shares the same data format as the Android Clipboard+ app, so your phone and PC sync bidirectionally through your own Google Drive (an app-private hidden folder) with no dedicated server. It captures clips via native Windows clipboard events without polling, and lets you organize text and images into groups and snippets and search across them.

Key Features

  • Tray-resident with poll-free real-time clipboard monitoring (Win32 events), auto-capturing text and images
  • Bidirectional phone-to-PC sync through your own Google Drive — no dedicated server, using only the drive.appdata scope
  • Android-style UI with light/dark themes, group sidebar, snippets, and pin/copy/edit/search
  • Multi-select bulk move, delete, and merge, newest/oldest sorting, and a full-size image viewer
  • Global hotkeys to open the window and instantly capture text selected in other apps, plus run-at-startup
  • Export/import compatible with the Android Clipboard+ backup format (text JSON, image ZIP)

FAQ

Does it sync with the Android Clipboard+ app?

Yes. Both apps share the same sync format and keep your phone and PC clipboards in sync bidirectionally through an app-private folder in your own Google Drive, with no dedicated server involved.

Where is my clipboard data stored, and is it safe?

Local data is stored in %APPDATA%\ClipboardPlus on your PC, and synced data lives only in an app-private hidden area of your own Google Drive. The app uses only the drive.appdata scope, so it never touches your regular Drive files.

What do I need to enable sync?

To turn on Google Drive sync, create a desktop OAuth client once in the Google Cloud Console, place the google_client.json file in the app folder, then click sync in the app to do a one-time Google sign-in and permission grant.

Word Cards+

Your child's first words — see them, hear them, and say them with cards.

View on Google Play → Contact us

Overview

Word Cards+ is a picture flashcard app that helps toddlers learn words in a fun way. With cute cards on familiar themes like animals, fruits, vehicles, and everyday objects, children can look at each picture, hear the word, and say it out loud. Clean white backgrounds and clear illustrations help little ones focus on each object and naturally grow their vocabulary.

Key Features

  • Themed flashcards for animals, fruits, vehicles, and everyday objects
  • Cute, clear illustrations that draw children's interest and focus
  • Listen-and-repeat learning with the spoken word on every card
  • Clean white backgrounds that keep the focus on each object
  • Simple, toddler-friendly controls for swiping through cards alone
  • Practical vocabulary built around familiar, everyday things

FAQ

What age is this app for?

It is made for toddlers who are starting to speak. They can learn words by looking at pictures, hearing the word, and repeating it out loud.

What word themes are included?

Flashcards cover familiar themes for young children, including animals, fruits, vehicles, and everyday objects.

Can my child use it alone?

Swiping through cards is simple enough for toddlers to view and listen on their own, and it works even better when a parent joins in.

ReadFocus+

A dyslexia & ADHD reading aid: line highlight and reading mask keep your eyes on track and ease eye strain.

View on Google Play → Contact us

Overview

ReadFocus+ is a reading ruler, reading mask and reading aid for dyslexia and ADHD on Android. Grant the overlay permission once and a translucent focus strip floats on top of any app — ebook readers, browsers, PDF, news, comics — keeping your eyes on the line you're reading. It reduces skipping or re-reading the same row, easing eye strain and boosting reading concentration and efficiency.

Key Features

  • Line Highlight (Reading Ruler): illuminates your current line to keep eyes on track
  • Reading Mask: dims everything except your reading zone for tunnel-vision focus
  • Fully adjustable focus strip: height (50dp–300dp), opacity (10%–100%), and corner roundness
  • 6 color themes (2 free + 4 Pro)
  • Works on top of any app — ebooks, PDF, webtoons, news, research papers
  • Pro: ad-free experience, unlimited overlay time, and keep screen on while reading

FAQ

How does ReadFocus+ work?

Grant the overlay permission once and a semi-transparent focus strip floats on top of any app. Drag it to the line you're reading and your eyes stay on track instead of wandering or skipping rows.

Which apps does it work with?

It works on top of any app on screen — ebook and PDF readers, research papers, web articles, blogs, news, study notes, textbooks, comics, webtoons, and long emails or chat history.

Is it free to use?

Yes. The free version gives 60 minutes of overlay time per session and 2 color themes. Pro adds an ad-free experience, all 6 themes, and unlimited overlay time — and you can also watch a short rewarded ad to unlock any Pro feature free for 24 hours.

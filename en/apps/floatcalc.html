FloatCalc+

A floating calculator that stays on top of any app so you never leave to do the math.

View on Google Play → Contact us

Overview

FloatCalc+ is a floating calculator that stays on top of any app, so you never have to switch screens just to do quick math. A built-in currency converter, unit converter, and quick memo all work right inside the overlay window. Drag the window wherever you need it, then copy results or save them to a memo with a single tap. It's lightweight, fast, and works offline (FX rates are cached daily).

Key Features

  • Floats over any app — YouTube, maps, browser, banking, shopping, spreadsheets
  • Draggable overlay window with one-tap copy of results to the clipboard
  • Unit converter for length, weight, temperature, area, volume, speed, and time
  • Real-time ECB exchange rates for 30+ currencies, refreshed daily
  • Quick memo to save calculation results or notes from the floating window
  • Choose a start mode (Calculator/Unit/FX/Memo), 8 themes, works offline

FAQ

Do I have to close my app every time I want to calculate?

No. FloatCalc+ works as a floating window that stays on top of other apps, so you can calculate without leaving your shopping, banking, or browser screen.

Where do the exchange rates come from?

It uses real-time ECB (European Central Bank) rates, refreshed daily. You can convert USD, EUR, JPY, KRW, CNY and 30+ world currencies right inside the overlay, and rates are cached each day so it works offline too.

How much can I use for free?

The free tier includes 5 sessions per day, up to 20 minutes each. One default theme is free, while 7 additional themes and some features are available with Pro.

FloatCrypto+

Keep the live Bitcoin price floating on your screen — 100% free.

View on Google Play → Contact us

Overview

FloatCrypto+ floats a real-time crypto ticker on top of any app — YouTube, Chrome, games, Telegram — so you can track BTC, ETH, SOL, XRP, DOGE, BNB and 1,000+ altcoins without ever switching apps. Powered by Binance market data with a CoinGecko fallback, it adds live charts, price alerts, and a watchlist in one place. It is 100% free with no login, no email, and no wallet required — ready to use in seconds.

Key Features

  • Floating overlay that keeps live Bitcoin and Ethereum prices on top of every app — no app switching
  • Drag to move, pinch to resize, with adjustable opacity and font size
  • Live 1D/7D/30D crypto charts powered by Binance data, with 24h change %
  • Instant price alerts with upper and lower thresholds and 24/7 background monitoring
  • Fast coin search across 1,000+ tokens with a real-time watchlist and price tracker
  • 100% free, no login or wallet needed, running as a lightweight, battery-friendly service

FAQ

Is FloatCrypto+ free? Do I need an account?

It is 100% free. There is no login, no email, and no wallet connection — just install and start tracking in seconds.

Which coins are supported and where does the data come from?

It supports Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, XRP, Dogecoin, BNB and over 1,000 altcoins, using Binance market data with a CoinGecko fallback for reliability.

Can I see prices while using other apps?

Yes. The floating overlay stays on top of every app, so you can watch live prices while using YouTube, playing games, or chatting — no app switching needed.

Clipboard+

Auto-save every copy with the smartest clipboard manager for Android

View on Google Play → Contact us

Overview

Clipboard+ is an Android clipboard manager that automatically saves every copied text and image in real time. A resizable floating overlay lets you reach your clipboard history from any screen without switching apps. Organize frequently used text into snippets and groups, and paste any clip fast with keyword search, a home screen widget, and a notification panel. You can also export your history to PDF and back up or restore your clips anytime.

Key Features

  • Auto-save copied text and images in real time
  • Resizable floating overlay for use over any app
  • Organize frequent text into snippets and groups
  • Instant search and filter by keyword, text, image, or date
  • Fast paste via widget, Quick Settings tile, and notification panel
  • Export clipboard history to PDF, plus backup and restore

FAQ

How is my clipboard history saved?

Every copied text and image is saved automatically in real time, so you can pull any clip back anytime without extra steps.

Can I access my clipboard history while using other apps?

Yes. A resizable floating overlay lets you reach your history from any screen without switching apps.

What is the difference between Free and Pro?

Free gives you the full manager with ads, while Pro removes ads, stores unlimited clipboard history, and unlocks all features. With FreePass you can watch an ad to unlock Pro features for 24 hours.

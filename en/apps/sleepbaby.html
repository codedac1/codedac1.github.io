SleepBaby+

Soothing lullabies and white noise to help your baby sleep.

View on Google Play → Contact us

Overview

SleepBaby+ is a lullaby and white noise app that helps your baby fall asleep peacefully. It offers calming sounds such as Brahms' Lullaby, gentle ocean waves, a flowing valley stream, and a crackling bonfire. A built-in sleep timer stops playback automatically after your chosen duration, and adjustable volume brings peaceful rest to both baby and parents.

Key Features

  • Warm classic lullabies including Brahms' Lullaby
  • Natural white noise such as ocean waves, valley stream, and bonfire
  • Sleep timer for 30 min, 45 min, 1, 2, 3 hours, or unlimited
  • Playback stops automatically when the timer ends
  • Easy volume adjustment
  • Built-in app sharing and feedback options

FAQ

What sounds does it play?

It offers soothing lullabies and white noise, including Brahms' Lullaby, gentle ocean waves, a valley stream, and a crackling bonfire.

Does it turn off automatically after a while?

Yes. You can set a sleep timer for 30 min, 45 min, 1, 2, or 3 hours, or unlimited, and playback stops automatically when the time is up.

Can I adjust the volume?

Yes. You can freely adjust the volume within the app to find the perfect level for helping your baby fall asleep.

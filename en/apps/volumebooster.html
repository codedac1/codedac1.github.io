VolumeBooster+

Make every app louder in real, measured decibels — not a meaningless 200%.

Overview

VolumeBooster+ is a volume booster that raises your entire system output — quiet speakers, low earphones, hard-to-hear videos, calls, music and games — so you finally hear them clearly. It boosts in true decibels (0 to +15 dB) rather than a meaningless "200%", and shows WHO-based Safe / Caution / Danger zones so you can turn things up while protecting your hearing. It combines a 5-band equalizer, bass booster, 3D virtualizer and AutoEQ headphone correction in a single app.

Key Features

  • Real-decibel volume boost (0 → +15 dB) with Safe/Caution/Danger labels, boosting every app system-wide
  • Loudness Maximizer plus an always-on peak limiter for genuinely louder sound with no clipping or distortion
  • 5-band Equalizer with 12 tuned presets (Pop, Rock, Vocal, Movie, Classical, Jazz, EDM, Gaming, Night and more)
  • Bass Booster + 3D Virtualizer for deeper, wider sound
  • AutoEQ headphone correction using measured frequency-response data
  • Hearing Health Score + WHO-based exposure monitoring, Sleep Timer, Channel Balance and Floating Widget (Pro)

FAQ

Does it really make my phone louder?

Yes. The Loudness Maximizer uses multiband compression and makeup gain to genuinely increase perceived loudness, and it raises the whole system output from 0 up to +15 dB — not just one app. It applies to music, videos, YouTube, games, calls and everything else.

How is this different from '200%' boosters?

Numbers like "200%" or "500%" that other boosters shout are meaningless. VolumeBooster+ shows the real gain in decibels and marks WHO-based Safe, Caution and Danger zones, so you always know exactly how loud you really are.

Is the displayed dB an exact measurement?

The decibel value is an estimate based on your system volume and the applied gain, and real loudness varies by device, headphones and content. The app is not a medical device, so if you notice ear pain or ringing, lower the volume and consult a professional.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications developed and provided by CodeDAC (“we”, “us”) (the “Apps”).

1. Core principle

Our Apps do not require you to create an account or sign in, and we do not collect or store personally identifiable information on our own servers. Most processing happens entirely on your device.

2. Information processed on your device

To provide app features, the following data is stored and processed only on your device and is not transmitted to us:

  • App data needed to provide features (for example, history, lists, and settings).
  • Content such as photos, videos and documents handled by some apps is never uploaded; it is processed locally on your device and encrypted where applicable.

3. Information collected by third-party services

To support our free apps and purchases, we use the following third-party services, which may collect information under their own policies:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — may collect your advertising identifier, coarse location, and device/usage information to deliver ads. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchases) — payments for PRO features are processed by Google Play; we do not store your payment method details.
  • Some apps connect to external data providers (e.g., public APIs) to display information, without sending any personal data.

4. Advertising and your choices

Free apps may include ads. You can limit personalized advertising by resetting your advertising ID or opting out of ad personalization in your device settings. Purchasing the PRO version removes ads.

5. Permissions

Each app requests only the permissions needed for its features and uses them solely for the stated purpose. Requested permissions vary by app, for example:

  • Display over other apps (overlay) — for floating calculator, timer, price ticker, etc.
  • Storage / media access — for editing and saving photos or documents.
  • Microphone — for audio features such as sound-level measurement (only in relevant apps).
  • Receive boot completed — for launching apps on startup.

6. Children’s privacy

For apps directed to children, we comply with the Google Play Families Policy and applicable laws. In child-directed contexts we do not use personalized advertising and do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data security and retention

On-device data is removed when you uninstall the app. Because we do not store personal data on our own servers, there is no separate server-side retention period.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect legal or service changes. Any changes will be posted on this page.
